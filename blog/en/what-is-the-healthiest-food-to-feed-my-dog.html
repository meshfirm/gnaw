What is the healthiest food to feed my dog?

12. July 2020 in dogfood

Before dog food was invented and before dogs were domesticated by humans they ate a healthy diet of fresh meat, bones and raw fruit and vegetables. Nowadays dogs are fed commercial dog food that is far different from what they originally ate. Slowly over time it has had a negative impact on the health of dogs all around the world from the posh Poodles in Paris to the hardworking Border Collies in New Zealand.

As well as being far from what nature intended, many dog food products today are full of processed meat, grains, fillers and additives that are unhealthy for your dog. So what should a poochy woochy be eating?

Fresh Dog food

We believe that fresh food is the best for your dog and is exactly what dogs need to eat to live the most healthy life possible. This diet is very similar to what dogs ate in the wild before being domesticated. It is full of fresh goodness, packed with all the good stuff that dogs need and want.

Changing to this fresh diet might significantly improve your dog’s overall health, improve energy levels, improve immunity and gut health, cure digestive problems, reduce allergies and help your dog maintain an ideal weight. Dental health could be improved because eating fresh food helps to clean dogs teeth naturally with less tartar buildup and gum health is also improved. These are just some of the health benefits we have discovered as there really are so many...less allergies, improved mood and behavior...the list goes on and on.

We believe your dog, like you, deserves the very best. Our products are organic, natural, healthy and let's not forget delicious. Your dog will love the new menu. An example is the chicken risotto that contains Finnish chicken meat, organs, rice, water, carrot, egg, peas, vitamins and minerals. Another popular product created especially for a healthy dog diet is the stew salmon. Every meal is a delicious treat that will send your dog onto the path of better health and improved vitality and wellness.

Talk to other dog owners to hear their real life experiences with changing to the fresh diet. First Hand experience is valuable and interesting. Most dog owners happily report they wouldn't consider going back after they see the positive life changes in their dog once they start dining on a fresh diet.

The benefits can be instant and you can expect to witness a change in your dog straight away. Certainly the benefits are long-lasting once you swap the food your dog is eating. If you compare the option of fresh food against processed and unnatural dog food it really is a no brainer. As we like to say - you wouldn't eat junk food every day so why should your dog?

You will personally feel much happier knowing you are caring for your dog in the best possible way by giving it the right diet it needs to thrive, not just survive. Every responsible and loving dog owner wants a happy and healthy dog. Show how much you love your precious pooch by giving it the best food fuel. After a taste of the fresh diet we are sure your dog will be wagging its tail all the way to dinner.
